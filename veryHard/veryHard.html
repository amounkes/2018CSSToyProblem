Born in Stratford-upon-Avon in 1564, Shakespeare's birthday is commonly observed on April 23rd, given that his name appears in the baptismal registry three days later on the 26th.

April 23rd, coincidentally, is also St. George's day (the patron saint of England), as well as the date of death for Willliam Shakespeare 52 years later in 1616.
